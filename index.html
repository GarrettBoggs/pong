Rules: If you enter a number greater than 0

Ping-Pong will count your number from 1 with the following exceptions

  1. Numbers divisble by 3 become "ping"
  2. Numbers divisble by 5 become "ping"
  3. Numbers divisble by 15 become "ping-pong"

Rules: If you enter a word

If you enter a word, it will output that word in an unordered list

  1. It will alert at a vowel
  2. It will have a custom alert for y
